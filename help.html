Arkanoid Game Instructions

Welcome to Arkanoid, the classic brick-breaking game!

Controls:

  • Left/Right Arrow: Move the paddle
  • Mouse/Touch: Move the paddle by following the cursor or touch
  • Space Bar: Launch the ball
  • 'S' Key: During the "shoot" power‑up (obtained by collecting a Gold capsule), fire special projectiles

Types of Bricks:

  • White, Green, Blue, Purple, Brown: Break with one hit.
  • Gray: Indestructible bricks – the ball always bounces.
  • Gold: Break with one hit and, with a defined probability, release power‑up capsules.
  • Copper: Require two hits; on first impact they change color (randomly) to indicate damage, on the second hit they break.

Power‑up Capsules:

  • The red capsule ("shoot") allows you to fire special projectiles (press S for 5 seconds).
  • The blue capsule ("paddleBoost") increases the paddle width by 30px for 5 seconds.

Complete all levels to finish the game and achieve the highest score.

Have fun and thank you for playing Arkanoid!
